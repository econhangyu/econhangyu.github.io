description: "Using 1.25 million online job postings from 2018–2024, we develop a task-based measure of exposure to large language models mapped into the U.S. SOC framework and adapted to the Chinese labor market. High-exposure occupations experienced slower employment growth after 2022, alongside rising employer requirements for education and experience and increased within-occupation inequality — patterns consistent with substitution pressures during an early period of AI adoption."
