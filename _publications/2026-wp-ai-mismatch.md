description: "Using 1.4 million vacancy–applicant pairs and matching outcomes from 2021–2025, we document that overall mismatch in the Chinese labor market has risen — but this trend reverses in occupations more directly exposed to AI after 2022. Mechanism evidence suggests that firms in these occupations adjusted job content, posting positions with a higher number of tasks and more complex skill bundles. These changes plausibly improve the clarity of job signals and reduce low-quality, opportunistic applications."
