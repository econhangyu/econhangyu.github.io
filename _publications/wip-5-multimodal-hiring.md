category: wip

description: "We leverage rich multimodal records — text responses, audio recordings, and videos — collected during the recruitment of more than 1,200 community health worker candidates in Mozambique. Machine-learning tools extract structured features from each modality, and we study when different signals diverge, interpreting divergence as evidence of information gaps invisible to standard survey or administrative measures. The goal is to evaluate whether multimodal measures improve prediction of subsequent job performance in resource-stressed service delivery systems."
